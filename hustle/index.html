Hustle.

Startups are known to always be on the move, hustling away and solving problems constantly. If you are like me, you will probably not mind waking up, and thinking an engineering problem through on a Saturday morning. The thought of hacking away at something always excites me. But hey, that's just me. And that's just the phase of life I'm currently in.

Who knows what the future holds? A couple of years from now and I might just want to wake up late, have a late brunch and party on a Saturday (hopefully there's no COVID then). That might be the case even a couple of months from now. Who knows? And who cares? We don't have to know for sure what we'll be doing in the future. That's too restrictive. Plans change more often than we'd like them to.

Focus on the present. Cause in a chaotic world, the present is all we can control, it is where we are and it is where we will ever be.
